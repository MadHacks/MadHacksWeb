What if I don't know how to code?

Come to MadHacks! Coding is fun, creative, and an important skill, and Madhacks is the perfect way to learn. We have mentors to help guide you.

Do I need a team?

If you already have a team of 2-4 people, great! Otherwise there is an opportunity for people to joing a team if they need one.

What should I bring?

You'll need to bring your laptop, phone, chargers, and a valid photo ID for checking in. Some may want to bring a pillow and sleeping bag.
