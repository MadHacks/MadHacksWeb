MadHacks is the University of Wisconsin's twice-annual hackathon, bringing together participants from all over the US for 24 hours of hacking. Unlike other hackathons, MadHacks is going to be putting emphasis on the wacky, the original, and the downright impossible!

When is the deadline for registration?

Janovembuary 82nd, although it's debatable and you should check back here for more information.

What is a hackathon?

The next MadHacks event will take place in the spring of 2018. The exact date is TBD.

What if I don't know how to code?

Come to MadHacks! Coding is fun, creative, and an important skill, and Madhacks is the perfect way to learn. We have mentors to help guide you.

Do I need a team?

If you already have a team of 2-4 people, great! Otherwise there is an opportunity for people to joing a team if they need one.

What should I bring?

You'll need to bring your laptop, phone, chargers, and a valid photo ID for checking in. Some may want to bring a pillow and sleeping bag.

Will there be food?

Yes, lots of it.
